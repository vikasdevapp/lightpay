LightPay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

LightPay is a self-custodial Web3 wallet application that runs locally on your Mac. We believe your private keys and data should remain solely on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email, private keys, seed phrases, or transaction history. We do not sell or share user data.

2. Local Storage

All wallet data and settings are stored encrypted on your Mac at ~/Library/Application Support/LightPay.

3. Contact

Email: privacy@lightpay.sh
