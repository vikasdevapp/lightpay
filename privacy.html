LightPay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

LightPay is a self-custodial Web3 wallet application designed with privacy-first principles. Your private keys and personal data remain solely on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email, private keys, seed phrases, or transaction history. We do not sell, rent, or share user data.

2. Local Storage

All wallet data and settings are securely stored in encrypted form on your Mac at ~/Library/Application Support/LightPay.

3. Security Commitment

LightPay prioritizes local encryption and secure storage practices to ensure your digital assets remain under your control.
