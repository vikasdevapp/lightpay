Getting Started

Install LightPay on macOS 11+ and use the plus (+) icon in the menu bar tray to add your first wallet account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LightPay free? Yes, it is fully open source under GPL-3.0.

Where are my keys? They are encrypted and stored locally on your Mac.

Security Tips

Always back up your seed phrase securely offline and never share private keys with anyone.
