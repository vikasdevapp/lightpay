Web3 Without the Friction

Browser extensions trap your Web3 access inside a single tab. LightPay frees it. Sitting quietly in your menu bar, LightPay gives every browser, terminal, and native application on your Mac a single trusted Web3 surface.

Built for Self-Custody

  • Your Keys Never Leave Your Mac: Data is encrypted locally. No cloud. No middleman.
  • Hardware Wallet Support: Ledger, Trezor, and Lattice1 support built-in.
  • No Hidden Fees: We never take a cut. Pay only network gas.
